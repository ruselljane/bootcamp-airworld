Baggage Allowance

You are given 7 kg carry-on that is 2 bags at most and 20 kg check-in baggage that is 2 luggage at most.

In addition to the free carry-on allowance, a passenger is allowed to carry the following items onboard:
- laptop with case
- small handbag
- coat
- wrap or blanket
- walking stick or a pair of crutches
- small camera or a pair of binoculars
- reasonable amount of reading materials infant's food and carrying basket

*Note that there is an additional $10 per kilogram fee for baggage exceed.
